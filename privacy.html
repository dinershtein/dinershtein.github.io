Privacy Policy for Chrome Extension

Last Updated: April 27, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how this Chrome extension handles data when you use it. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal information or user data through our extension.

Our extension operates entirely on your device and does not:

- Collect personal information

- Track your browsing activity

- Store your search history

- Access your browsing history

- Send any data to our servers or third parties

- Use cookies or similar tracking technologies

Data Storage

All functionality of this extension is processed locally on your device. No user data is stored on external servers.

Third-Party Services

Our extension does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services or analytics tools.

Changes to the Extension

In the event that future versions of our extension change how data is handled, we will update this Privacy Policy and notify users accordingly before implementing any changes that affect data collection or processing.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Chrome Web Store and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our extension, please contact us through the support information provided on our Chrome Web Store listing.

Legal Compliance

This extension complies with the Google Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies and applicable data protection laws.
